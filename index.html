    Projects

    School Tutoring

    I initiated the "School Tutoring" project to support public school students in Brazil who faced educational disruptions during the COVID-19 pandemic. We provided personalized online classes, covering subjects as per student needs. This project was recognized by the institution’s president, contributing to students' educational continuity during a challenging time.

    ENGG 200 - Portable Video Game Console

    In my first year of Engineering, I collaborated with a team to design a portable video game console inspired by "Piano Tiles." We implemented four songs and a user-friendly design, marking my first practical application of theoretical engineering principles using Fusion360 for the design.

    SUSE 300 - Sustainable Transit Redesign

    Our group project focused on redesigning Calgary's urban transit system to make it more sustainable and accessible. We proposed an upgraded bus station model, integrating green materials and community hubs, inspired by discussions with professionals from Arup.

    SUSE 315 - Engineering Economics and Decision Making for Sustainability

    In SUSE 315, our final project was to evaluate the installation of a rainwater cistern at MacKimmie Tower using triple bottom line accounting. This project highlighted the challenges of balancing environmental benefits with economic feasibility, showcasing the importance of holistic thinking in sustainable engineering.
